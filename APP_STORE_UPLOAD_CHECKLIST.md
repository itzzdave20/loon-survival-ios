# App Store / TestFlight Upload Checklist

## Developer setup

- Apple Developer account is active.
- Xcode is installed and updated.
- Signing team is selected in Xcode.
- Bundle Identifier is unique.

## App information

- App name: Loon Survival
- Category: Games
- Version: 1.0.3
- Build number: 1
- Orientation: Landscape

## Required assets

- App icon: 1024 × 1024 px PNG, no transparency
- iPhone screenshots
- App preview video, optional
- Support URL
- Privacy policy URL

## Testing before upload

- Game opens without crash.
- Touch controls work.
- Audio works.
- Landscape orientation works.
- No missing textures.
- No missing CSV/map files.
- App restarts cleanly.

## Upload steps

1. Open the Xcode project.
2. Select **Any iOS Device**.
3. Click **Product > Archive**.
4. Open Organizer.
5. Click **Distribute App**.
6. Select **App Store Connect**.
7. Upload.
8. Open App Store Connect.
9. Add build to TestFlight.
10. Submit for review after testing.
